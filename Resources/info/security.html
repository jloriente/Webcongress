LIFE FESTIVAL is a limited Event. Getting this special License will allow us to enjoy more hours of music during the night and have a relaxed festival.

Gatecrashers can put the Festival life at Risk.

Gardai is aware of this and this has forced us to implement a security plan to provide a safe Event to our LIFERS.

Learn the lesson form previous years. Security levels will be much more efficient particularly outside the event and on the perimeter to provide, as reassurance, a presence in the local area and to deal with potential crime. In the arena, our security will be friendly, but firm. Use of CCTV and a new In/out system with checking wristbands points in camping entry / exit and arenas will be implemented.

Some of the new security improvements are Compacting Festival area Creating a full vision perimeter visibility High Level Security Wristbands.

Strategic Security Positions and Wristbands control checking points. This year the Camping will be fenced and secured. with vigilant towers and the entrance/ exits to the Camping will be Wristband checked.

Working on your security to have a safe Life.
